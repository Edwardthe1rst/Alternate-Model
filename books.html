Compilation Chart of the Bible's Books A section-by-section survey of books, chapters, figures, and themes

These following tables present a structured overview of the books of the Bible, organized by western traditional sections. Each table covers one section and lists, for every book, the key chapters, the principal characters, the major events recorded, and the overarching theological themes at work. This chart is intended as a reference aid - a starting point for study rather than a substitute for reading the texts themselves.
